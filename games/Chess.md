## Checkmate patterns

### Back-Rank Mate

Use a Rook or a Queen to trap the King between the edge of the board and his friendly pieces.

![](http://www.fen-to-image.com/image/30/single/4R1k1/5ppp/8/8/8/8/8/7K)
